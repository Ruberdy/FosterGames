LifeAfter Season 4: The 2nd Outbreak Launches! Biggest Zombie Swarm Ever Approaches!

2022-11-02 share:

     LifeAfter Season 4: The 2nd Outbreak Launches Today

Biggest Zombie Swarm Ever and New Gameplay “Battle for Survival” Available

The most popular Open World Doomsday Survival Game LifeAfter has launched its new version -- Season 4: The 2nd Outbreak on November 4, 2021. Levin City is under siege by the biggest zombie swarm ever! In a time when things are getting out of control and strange phenomena are taking place everywhere in the doomsday world, survivors must defend the city and safeguard their hard fought homeland! The update comes with the debut of heavy vehicle Iron Stride-54 and new gameplay Battle for Survival, as well as rich rewards for players such as limited Facelift card, commemorative outfit and furniture.

LifeAfter is an Open World Doomsday Survival Game which has 200 million registered players worldwide since its launch in 2018. Players are facing scarce resources, harsh weather, and dangerous wild lives, and from scratch collect resources, build houses, make tools and weapons, and fight alongside with teammates to make and defend a home in a post-apocalyptic world.

 

Biggest Zombie Sward Ever, Levin City Siege Battle Begins

In Season 4, players first have to face a zombie swarm of unprecedented scale. Countless new Infected will be attacking Levin City! This horrifying shift requires every survivor to unite to defend and retake Levin.

Zombies swarm is one of the key features of the zombie apocalypse genre. Our update this time will be focusing on that as well—besides the swarms you’ll encounter in stages, they’ll show up during map exploration, creating a compelling visual and battling impact. The dev team has optimized the Infected, making them more realistic and affording them more features. For example, the life they had before they turned zombie will have an impact on their behaviors—athletes more agile and lawyers more prudent. These optimizations aim to arouse fear in players just like they first came to the doomsday world—an instinct fear when facing death and corpse.

 

In addition, a great number of machinery of war will be introduced for the first time in this update, such as tanks and helicopter gunships, and tactical gears like turrets and rocket launchers. Survivors will be able to operator these weapons to lay suppressive fire on the Infected and take back Levin City!

 

New Gameplay “Battle for Survival”, Witness the Epic Battle

The Season 4 comes with a new gameplay called Battle for Survival. Different from the concept “stages” which everyone has been familiar with, in Battle for Survival players will go through a battle that lasts for 14 days! There will be different quests and storyline development each day and as the protagonists of the battle, every choice and action of survivors will make an actual impact on the doomsday survival. These two Battle for Survival will first be faced by survivors in this update:

Mass Crisis: Black orbs of unknown nature appeared all around the world. Survivors who go to investigate are attacked by new Infected near the orbs. What will they do when teammate turns Infected and how will they counter these unknown orbs?

Dusk of Levin: After the Mass Crisis, a lot of pollen appeared around the world. Survivors follow the source and end up coming to Levin City. Infected grow stronger by exposing to pollen and the sudden eclipse. Survivors need to rescue refugees while fighting pollen Infected, and find out the last clue.

 

Prepare for the Season 4, Rich Bonus Waiting for You

To celebrate the launch of Season 4: The 2nd Outbreak, the operation team has prepared rich bonus for players to better cope with the new unknown threats! Starting now till November 17, 2021, for 14 days, log in the game each day to claim rewards! Including: all kinds of materials, Formula Shard, limited Facelift Card, Season 4 commemorative outfit, furniture and pendants. Don’t miss out!

 

 

The world of LifeAfter enters a new chapter in the Season 4. In the face of massive zombie swarms, how will survivors choose between offense and defense? The new game mode Battle for Survival also brings constant challenges. As a survivor of the doomsday world, show your courage and defend your homeland!

About LifeAfter

LifeAfter is an open-world doomsday survival game developed by NetEase Games (both iOS and Android version). Players are facing scarce resources, harsh weather, and dangerous wild lives, and from scratch collect resources, build houses, make tools and weapons, and fight alongside with teammates to make and defend a home in a post-apocalyptic world. By virtue of its vivid rendition of a doomsday world and survival struggles, the game now has 200 million registered players worldwide since its launch in 2018. It was dubbed the Most Competitive Game of 2019 by Google Play.
